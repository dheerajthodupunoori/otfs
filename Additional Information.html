Cash Deposits

If you had cash deposits of more than 2 lakh between 9th Nov 2016 and 30th Dec 2016, you need to mention the amount deposited here.

You should mention the amount of deposit in each bank account separately.

You should mention this even if individual deposits were less than 2 lakh Rupees, but your total deposits (across multiple bank accounts too) are over 2 lakh.
